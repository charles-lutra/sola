Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Can I install this in a rented flat? +
Yes. Under the Renters' Rights Act 2025, landlords in England cannot unreasonably refuse improvements that don't structurally modify the property. Our drill-free railing clamp leaves zero marks. Always notify your landlord in writing first — we provide a template letter.
Do I need an electrician? +
No. Under BS 7671 Amendment 4 (April 2026), certified plug-in solar kits under 800W can be connected to a standard 13A UK socket by any household member. No professional required.
What happens when I move? +
You take it with you. The entire kit is portable — unclamp the bracket, unplug the cable, pack the panels in the original box. Set up again at your new place in under an hour. This is the whole point.
How does the subscription work? +
Pay £29/month (Sola 400) for 18 months, then you own it outright. We use Klarna — no credit check required for most customers. Cancel within 30 days for a full refund if you're not satisfied.
What if my panel breaks? +
All Sola kits come with a 10-year panel warranty and 12-year microinverter warranty. If anything fails within that period, we'll send a replacement component directly to you, free of charge.
